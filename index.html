We Are Maplewood

SINCE 2006

Maplewood Hotel boasts of wide range of state of the art facilities that will make you want to come back today, tomorrow and always.

Facilities include cocktail bar, conference hall with a seating capacity of 20 with internet access, a spacious car park, restaurant and accommodation with hot and cold water.

We offer comfort, refinement and a unique character that will make your stay here unforgettable.

It is a place to rest and relax, to indulge in fine food and drinks, and enjoy the fabled warmth of Ghanaian hospitality.

We subscribe to the belief that the way to provide our guests with the finest residential experience is to set the standards quality and maintain them with unceasing rigor, treating their requests and desires as absolute necessities at all times
